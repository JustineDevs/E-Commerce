## Policy text

**Return and Exchange Policy**

We accept returns or exchanges for eligible apparel items that are defective, damaged, incorrectly delivered, or materially different from the product ordered. For eligible non-defective items, we may allow size exchange within 7 days from receipt, provided the item is unused, unwashed, unaltered, with original tags, packaging, and proof of purchase.

Items reported as defective, damaged, or not as described may qualify for replacement, exchange, or refund after inspection. Philippine consumer rules recognize repair, replacement, or refund as valid remedies for defective products.

## Eligible cases

You may request a return, exchange, or refund if:
- The wrong item, size, or color was sent.
- The item arrived damaged or has a manufacturing defect.
- The item is substantially different from the product listing.
- The parcel was lost in transit or confirmed undelivered after carrier investigation.

Online sellers in the Philippines are expected to clearly disclose refund and exchange procedures, and defective or misdescribed items may justify a refund or replacement.

## Non-returnable items

The following are not eligible for return or exchange unless defective or incorrectly shipped:
- Used, washed, stained, or altered items.
- Items without original tags or proof of purchase.
- Clearance or final-sale items, if clearly marked as such before purchase.
- Intimate or hygiene-sensitive items such as underwear, socks, stockings, swimwear bottoms, or pierced accessories.

Hygiene-based exclusions for intimate apparel and similar items are common retail practice, but defective items should still be handled with an appropriate remedy.

## Process and timeline

To request a return or exchange, contact customer support within **7 days of delivery** and provide:
- Order number.
- Reason for request.
- Clear photos or video of the item and packaging.
- Preferred resolution: exchange, replacement, store credit, or refund.

For wrong, damaged, or defective items, we recommend committing to review within **2 to 5 business days** after complete evidence is received, then issue the approved resolution after inspection. For defective goods, consumers should notify the seller as soon as the defect is discovered.

## Refunds and shipping

If the return is approved because the item is defective, damaged, incorrect, or not as described, the customer should receive a replacement, exchange, or refund, and the business should not pass the remedy cost to the customer for a valid defect claim. If the request is for a non-defective size exchange, you may state that return shipping is customer-paid unless you choose to subsidize it as a customer service policy.

You can use this simple refund rule:
- **Seller fault**: full refund or free replacement/exchange.
- **Customer size change**: exchange only, customer handles return shipping.
- **No stock available for exchange**: refund or store credit.

Here is a short storefront-ready version:

Approved seller-fault claims qualify for replacement, exchange, or refund.
